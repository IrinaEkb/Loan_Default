# Approval Policy Simulation

## Objective

Evaluate the financial impact of expanding loan approvals to higher-risk borrowers.

Using predicted probabilities of default generated by the Logistic Regression model, this analysis estimates how changes in approval policy may affect:

* Expected number of defaults
* Expected financial losses
* Portfolio risk exposure

## Business Question

What happens if the bank approves an additional 10% of high-risk loan applicants?

The goal is to quantify the trade-off between portfolio growth and increased credit risk.

## Approach

Loan applicants are ranked by predicted probability of default.

The highest-risk segment is identified and used to simulate an expanded approval policy.

Expected losses are estimated using:

Expected Loss = Probability of Default × Loan Amount

The results are compared against the current approval strategy to assess potential business impact.
